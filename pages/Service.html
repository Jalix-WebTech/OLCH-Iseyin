Our Medical Services

Reliable, patient-centered care delivered

professionals

General Consultation

Professional medical consultation and diagnosis for patients.

Our doctors provide detailed evaluation, diagnosis and medical advice to ensure proper treatment and patient wellbeing.

HAART to Heart

Specialized HIV treatment and counseling program.

Our HAART program provides testing, treatment, monitoring and counseling services to support individuals living with HIV.

Cervical Cancer Screening

Early detection screening for cervical cancer.

This screening service helps detect early cervical abnormalities to promote early treatment and prevent complications.

Laboratory

Reliable medical laboratory testing services.

Our laboratory provides accurate diagnostic testing using modern equipment and professional laboratory scientists.

Pharmacy

Safe and reliable medication services.

Our pharmacy dispenses quality medications prescribed by doctors with guidance from qualified pharmacists.

Surgeries

Professional surgical procedures.

Our surgical unit provides both minor and major operations performed by trained surgeons using safe medical procedures.

Ambulance

Emergency patient transport service.

Our ambulance service ensures rapid patient transportation with basic emergency life support equipment.

Pediatrics

Healthcare services for infants and children.

Our pediatric department provides diagnosis, treatment and preventive healthcare for infants and children.

Ante-Natal Care

Comprehensive pregnancy monitoring services.

Our antenatal care program supports mothers with routine checkups, counseling and pregnancy monitoring.

Ultrasound Scan

Advanced imaging for internal body diagnosis.

Our ultrasound imaging technology helps doctors detect and monitor medical conditions safely and accurately.

Emergency

24/7 emergency medical care services.

Our emergency unit operates around the clock to handle urgent medical conditions and accidents.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and physical therapy treatments.

Our physiotherapy services assist patients recovering from injury, surgery, or illness through therapeutic exercises.

Mortuary

Respectful preservation of the deceased.

Our mortuary services ensure professional and respectful handling and preservation of deceased individuals.
