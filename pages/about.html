About Us

Our Lady Catholic Hospital Iseyin is a leading healthcare institution committed to delivering compassionate, ethical, and comprehensive medical services to our community. With a strong legacy of excellence and service, we continue to uphold the highest standards of patient-centered care.

Our hospital is equipped with modern medical facilities and supported by a dedicated team of experienced healthcare professionals. We provide a wide range of services including emergency care, outpatient and inpatient services, surgical procedures, and specialized medical treatments — all within a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment.

Our Priorities

Patient Safety

Ensuring the highest safety standards in all medical procedures and care delivery.

Quality Care

Providing evidence-based treatments and continuous improvement in healthcare services.

Community Health

Promoting wellness initiatives and outreach programs to support public health.

Innovation

Embracing modern technology and medical advancements to enhance patient outcomes.
