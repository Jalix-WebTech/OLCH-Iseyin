About Us

Our Lady Catholic Hospital Iseyin is a leading healthcare institution committed to delivering compassionate, ethical, and comprehensive medical services to our community. With a strong legacy of excellence and service, we continue to uphold the highest standards of patient-centered care.

Our hospital was established on 1st November, 1970, accredited by Oyo State Ministry of health and operates on daily basis.

Our Collaborators

The hospital collaborates with several Non-Governmental Organizations to provide quality healthcare services:

APIN Public Initiative

Provides free HIV/AIDS counselling, testing, and treatment. Also supports staff training, workshops, and periodic grants for hospital programs.

Damien Foundation & Catholic Caritas Nigeria

Partners in tuberculosis care. The hospital serves as a DOT center for free TB testing and treatment.

National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS)

Accredited provider for primary and secondary healthcare including surgery, obstetrics & gynecology, radiology, and lab services.

Oyinbo Fashola Ifa Foundation

Offers free basic medical tests and treatments to the community annually.

Monitor Health Care

Supported subsidized breast screening for over 100 women, promoting early detection and care.

These partnerships have greatly strengthened the hospital’s services over the years.
