Our Departments

Expert care across specialized departments
with advanced facilities and
professionals.

Outpatient Department (OPD)

The OPD provides consultation, diagnosis, and treatment for patients who do not require hospital admission. Our doctors ensure efficient and quality medical care for all outpatient visits.

Accounting Department

Our accounting department manages financial transactions, billing services, and insurance processes to ensure transparency and efficient financial management within the hospital.

Pharmacy

The pharmacy department provides safe and effective medication services while ensuring proper drug dispensing, storage, and patient counseling.

Maternity Department

Our maternity department offers comprehensive care for mothers before, during, and after childbirth with experienced midwives and obstetricians.

Laboratory

The laboratory department provides accurate diagnostic testing using modern equipment to support effective treatment and patient care.

Health Information Management

This department manages patient records, data privacy, medical documentation, and information systems to ensure accurate healthcare data management.
